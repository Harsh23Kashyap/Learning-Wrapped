The Harappan civilization boasted sophisticated urban planning.  Mohenjo-daro's impressive drainage system reached every home, featuring private bathrooms and courtyards.  Wells were common in Kalibangan.  Fortified cities like Dholavira and Lothal showcased advanced urban design with internal walls dividing sections.  Agriculture thrived in flood-plain settlements, yielding plentiful wheat, barley, peas, and other crops, showcasing a self-sufficient food system.  This evidence paints a picture of a highly organized and advanced civilization.
